Power Trace Kit

Power Trace Kit helps teams inspect and compare low-power consumption behavior during firmware iterations. It pairs capture presets with repeatable reporting layouts.

What was built

  • Repeatable measurement routines for lab benches.
  • Comparative traces across firmware commits.
  • Simple report template for design review discussions.

Result

Design loops moved faster because power regressions became visible earlier in development.
