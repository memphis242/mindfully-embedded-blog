Firmware Canary Lab

Firmware Canary Lab is a release safety pattern for constrained edge devices. It stages changes with quick rollback points and validates behavior with targeted observability checks.

What was built

  • Canary rollout stages tuned for unreliable links.
  • Health gates for boot stability, watchdog resets, and power profile drift.
  • Rollback automation when thresholds are exceeded.

Result

Release confidence improved while protecting field fleets from broad regressions.
